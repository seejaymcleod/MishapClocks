## How to Use
Rather than using traditional mishap tables, roll twice on the Materia Mishap circle: once for the **Mishap School**, and once for the **Mishap Effect **
1) **Determine Spell School:** Go by GM or Player feel. Creativity is key, not balance.
2) Roll **2d8** Simultaneously:
   
### 1st d8: Mishap School
*Consult this table **(not the circle)** to determine the elemental flavor of the mishap:*

| **d8 Roll** | **School Result**                                        |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| 6-8         | Cast school *(same polarity)*                            |
| 4-5         | Cast school *(inverted polarity)*                        |
| 3           | Circle-opposite School (positive)                        |
| 2           | Circle-opposite School (negative)                        |
| 1           | Cast school *(same)* + Random **d8** school *(inverted)* |

### 2nd d8: Mishap Effect
*Look at that specific numbered slice on the **Mishap Circle** to determine the effect.*
- The **Spell Tier (T0–T5)** determines the specific ring and its **Effect Modifications**.
- The **DC** and **dice** tracks on the wheel are for reference, to be used as desired.

## Rolling Doubles - AXIS FRACTURE
When a mishap roll results in **doubles**, the cosmic circuit snaps. The spell fails catastrophically.

- **Tier Spike:** Increase the spell's **Tier by +1**.    
- **Dual Schools:** Use the **circle** to trigger **both** the rolled school (Positive polarity) and opposed school (Negative polarity).    
- **The Chaos Die:** Roll a **third d8** to determine the mishap's **Geometry (Shape)**.    
    - **Resonance:** If the third d8 matches the doubled number, the die **explodes**. Increase the **Tier by +1** and **re-roll** it.        
    - **Past Tier 5:** If an explosion happens at Tier 5, add **+1 instance of effect dice** and **+2 to the DC** instead. Re-roll. Repeat this process until a unique number is rolled.        
- **Dual Shapes:** Once a unique number is settled, trigger **both** that geometry shape and its visual opposite on the circle at the final escalated Tier.

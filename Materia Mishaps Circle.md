## Core Procedure & Catastrophes

1. **Determine Spell School:** Go by GM or Player narrative feel. Creativity is key, not balance.

2. **Roll 2d8 Simultaneously:** The **School Die** and the **Effect Die**. Resolve on the tables below unless overridden by one of the following conditions:

- **1's Cascade:**
    - _School Die is 1:_ Select **School Cascade**. Resolve Cast School (same polarity) + roll d8 for a random School (inverted polarity). Explodes on a 1.
    - _Effect Die is 1:_ Select **Primal Chaos**. Resolve the tier effect + roll d8 for a random Effect. Explodes on a 1.
      
- **Doubles (Axis Fracture):** The first d8 selects the rolled school (same polarity) and its circle-opposite (inverted polarity). The second d8 **explodes** to select both the rolled effect and its circle-opposite effect.
  
- **Double 1s (Total Collapse):** Perform both **School Cascade** and **Primal Chaos** Effect as above, but the additional rolls both automatically explode, increasing the spell tier by +2. 

### Exploding & Overcharge

- **Explode:** Increase the spell tier by **+1** and re-roll the triggering die. Explode again if the result is another 1.
- **Tier 5+ Overcharge:** When exploding a die at spell tier 5+, instead of increasing the tier, add **+2 to the spell DC** and **+1 instance of effect dice**.
